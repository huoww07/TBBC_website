About Us

We are scientists who are busy trying to figure out what we want to be when we grow up.

In between experiments, we think about what careers we want to pursue, and who is hiring bright, talented, business-minded people like ourselves. Members of the Tufts Biomedical Business Club benefit from the many educational resources, seminars, events and opportunities for career development that we organize. In order to to continue to deliver high-quality content to young scientists and to ensure the long-term sustainability of the club, we are seeking sponsorship and partnerships.

We’ll invest the time if you invest the money.

TBBC Initiatives

Biotech Journal Club: 
Monthly Student presentations/Discussions of hot topics in the biotech world. 2015 - 2016 topics included: 
Moderna Therapeutics, FDA Special Tracks, Improving pharma R&D, Europe's Innovative Medicines Initiative, Google's Biotech Arm, and the CRISPR patent bottle.

Biotech Buzz: 
Monthly informal coffee hour discussions with prominent guests, such as, Frank Rimalovski, Executive Director of NYU's Innovation Venture Fund.

Consulting

Case Study Group: 
Weekly meetins where students and postdocs interested in consulting practice solving cases together.

Tufts Advisory Partners:
Pro bono consulting services.
A rigorous program that offers graduate students and postdocs in-depth, hands-on experience in consulting and leadership.

Tufts New England Case Competition (TUNECC):
Yearly contest in which ADCs from the Northeast compete in structured problem solving for the life sciences industry.

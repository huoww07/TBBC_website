TBBC is pleased to offer two new online certifications:

Fundamentals of Business - COMING SOON!

This online course aims to give biomedical scientists a fundamental understanding of business terminology, functions, and operations. Topics in this course include an introduction to business concepts, marketing & finance basics, and basic business operations. This course requires access to LinkedIn learning (provided to all Tufts users). Complete this course to receive TBBC's Fundamentals of Business Certificate. A guided version of this course will be offered in early 2021.

Career Basics - COMING SOON!

This course aims to give scientists the fundamental skills needed for getting a job outside of academia. Topics include resume/CV building, networking, and interviewing techniques, among others. This course requires access to LinkedIn Learning (provided to all Tufts users). Complete this course to receive TBBC's Career Basics Certificate. A guided version of this course will be offered in early 2021.

About Us

The mission of the Tufts Biomedical Business Club (TBBC) is tocultivate business leaders in the health and life sciences industries.  We aim to increase business literacy, learnfrom biotech leaders, and promote the professional development of our members. 
TBBC hosts career development and exploration events to helpstudents identify career interests and prepare for careers in business,biotech, consulting, and more. Through our events, TBBC allows students to takean active role in networking for their future careers. We are activelyrecruiting students and postdocs interested in gaining leadership experience,networking with industry leaders, and preparing for a career in biotech,consulting, venture capital, and so much more.

Our History

The Tufts Biomedical Business Club (TBBC) was founded in 2008 by Tufts GSBS PhD candidates wanted to explore the interface between business and science. We grew into what is now a large and vibrant community of graduate, medical, dental, and nutrition students, postdocs, physicians, scientists and alumni.

In the rapidly evolving biotech landscape we provide our members with opportunities to learn about consulting, entrepreneurship, business development, IP and more.
